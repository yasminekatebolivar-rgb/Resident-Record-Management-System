Barangay Resident Record Management System

Digital • Centralized • Secure

A simple admin-only system for managing resident & household information.

Overview

The Barangay Resident Record Management System (BRRMS) is a digital platform designed to help barangay staff efficiently record, organize, and manage resident and household information. It replaces paper forms and spreadsheets with a centralized and structured database.

Intended use: Single-user / Admin-only (authorized barangay personnel).

Key Features

🧾 Resident Information

Add, view, update, and manage complete resident records efficiently.

🏠 Household Profiling

Group residents by household, identify family heads, and track members.

🔎 Search & Filter

Find residents easily by name, age, or address with quick filtering options.

🏷️ Categorization

Tag residents as Senior Citizen, PWD, Student, Voter/Non-voter, etc.

📊 Reports & Statistics

Generate population reports, household counts, and age distributions.

🔒 Admin Access

Restricted system access for authorized barangay personnel only.

Expected Outcomes

  • Centralized and secure resident data storage
  • Faster retrieval and processing of information
  • Accurate reporting for planning and disaster response
  • Reduced duplicate or incorrect records
  • Improved transparency and service delivery
